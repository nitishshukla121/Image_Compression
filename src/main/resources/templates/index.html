About ImageCompressor

Our free image compressor is built to be simple and powerful. We believe in providing a tool that just works, without any unnecessary steps or sign-ups.

  • **Good Quality Compression:** Our smart algorithm optimizes your image for the best balance of size and quality.
  • **No Sign-up or Fees:** This tool is completely free to use. Just drag, drop, and compress.
  • **Drag & Drop:** Easily upload your image by dragging and dropping it anywhere on the page.

Our tool currently supports JPEG, PNG, and WebP formats, providing you with high-quality results every time.
